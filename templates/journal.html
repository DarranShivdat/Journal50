Some Questions to Consider While Journaling:

  • How was my day?
  • What am I proud of accomplishing today?
  • What is something fortunate that happened today?
  • Who did I talk to today?
  • What made me happy today?
  • What can I do better tomorrow?

Questions

How was your day? (1-10)
How would you rate your mood today? (1 is very sad and 10 is extremely happy)
How would you rate your social interaction? (1 represents being alone and 10 represents being very social)
How well did you sleep? (1 represents no good sleep and 10 represents being well rested)
How much was your work load today? (1 represents unsatisfactory amounts of work and 10 represents perfect amount of work)
How was your appetite? (1 represents poor appetite and 10 represents a strong appetite)
How well did you concentrate? (1 represents no concentration and 10 represents being highly concentrated)
